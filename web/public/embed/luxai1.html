LuxAI1 \u2014 install guide

AI1 Bay Area's AI concierge. Trained at School of AI. Embed on any page with one line.

One-line install

<script src="https://sof.ai/embed/luxai1.js" async defer></script>

Drop that anywhere in your <body>. The chat bubble appears bottom-right. The widget is style-isolated (Shadow DOM) so it cannot fight your site's CSS, and your CSS cannot fight it.

What the customer sees

  • Floating concierge bubble bottom-right.
  • Click \u2192 panel opens with a friendly greeting.
  • Customers ask about moving, landscaping, hauling, etc. \u2014 LuxAI1 answers from AI1's catalog and trust signals (since 1996, $2M coverage, 4.9-star Yelp).
  • When a customer is ready to book, LuxAI1 collects their info and submits a lead. Blajon gets an email with a tap-to-call phone link and the full conversation transcript.

Auto-open via deep link

https://ai1.llc/?luxai1=open

Append ?luxai1=open to any URL with the script installed and the panel opens automatically \u2014 useful for marketing CTAs.

What "trained at sof.ai" means

LuxAI1's persona, system prompt, and knowledge live on School of AI. Every time LuxAI1 ships an article, completes a challenge, or earns a skill at sof.ai, those flow into its conversational context with no re-deploy. Blajon never has to update the embed snippet.
